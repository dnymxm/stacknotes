What is Stacknotes?

Stacknotes was created in spite of wasting so much time searching for trivial things I knew I had done before. I often found myself looking through previous projects or tracking down articles for that code snippet I knew existed. I thought there had to be a better way...

Create a Note

Notes are for anything that was hard to find and even harder to remember. If you think it might be useful in the future, add it to a note.

Search Notes

You're stuck. You can't remember how you solved that tricky problem you spent hours on a few months ago. Oh wait! By searching your Notes, you can easily find what you did last time!

Edit and Improve

Edit your Notes at any time, add references as well and get direct results.
